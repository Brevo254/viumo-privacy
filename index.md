# Privacy Policy for Viumo

Last updated: May 2026

Welcome to Viumo. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your information while providing a smooth entertainment experience.

## Information We Collect

Viumo may collect limited account-related information when users register or sign in, including:
- Email address
- Username or display name
- Notification preferences
- Room participation and creation data

This information is used strictly to support app functionality and improve user experience.

## Analytics

Viumo uses anonymous analytics to understand app performance, detect crashes, and improve user experience. This information is not used to identify any individual user.

## Internet Usage

The app requires an internet connection to stream video content. No personal data is stored during this process.

## Data Sharing

Viumo does not sell, trade, or share user data with any third parties.

## Purpose of Data

Any anonymous technical data collected is strictly used to:
- Improve playback performance
- Fix crashes and bugs
- Enhance overall app stability

## Internet Usage

An internet connection is required for streaming content, syncing rooms, notifications, and certain app features.

## Notifications

If enabled by the user, Viumo may send notifications related to:
- Content updates
- Room activity
- Recommendations
- Important app announcements

Users can manage or disable notifications at any time within the app settings.

## Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect user information and prevent unauthorized access. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

## Data Sharing

Viumo does not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties.

User information is only used for operating and improving the application.

## User Control

Users may request account-related assistance or deletion requests through our contact email below.

## Children's Privacy

Viumo is not intended for children under the age of 13 without parental guidance.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as Viumo evolves and new features are introduced. Continued use of the app after updates means acceptance of the revised policy.
